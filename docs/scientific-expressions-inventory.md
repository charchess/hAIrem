## 1. Facial Expression Taxonomy (FACS Based)
This catalog extends the basic Ekman set using the research on compound emotions (Du et al., 2014).

| Category | Expression Name | Action Units (AUs) | Visual Markers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic** | Happiness | AU 6+12 | Raised cheeks, crow's feet, upturned mouth. |
| **Basic** | Sadness | AU 1+4+15 | Inner brows raised, lowered mouth corners. |
| **Basic** | Fear | AU 1+2+4+5+7+20+26 | Brows raised/flat, upper eyelids raised, mouth stretched. |
| **Basic** | Anger | AU 4+5+7+23 | Brows lowered/drawn, tensed eyelids, pressed lips. |
| **Basic** | Surprise | AU 1+2+5+26 | Arched brows, wide eyes, dropped jaw. |
| **Basic** | Disgust | AU 9+15+16 | Wrinkled nose, raised upper lip, lowered mouth corners. |
| **Basic** | Contempt | AU R12 / R14 | Asymmetrical mouth tighten (one side). |
| **Compound** | Happily Surprised | AU 1+2+12+25 | Wide eyes, high brows, joyful smile. |
| **Compound** | Angrily Disgusted | AU 4+9+10 | Intense brow furrow + nose wrinkle. |
| **Compound** | Fearfully Surprised | AU 1+2+5+20 | Extreme eye widening + horizontal mouth stretch. |
| **Compound** | Appalled | AU 1+2+5+9 | High brows + nose wrinkle + upper lip lift. |

## 2. Body Attitudes & Kinesics (Birdwhistell & Mehrabian)
Taxonomy for body posture and non-verbal communication.

| Attitude Category | Theory | Description | Visual Implementation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High Immediacy** | Mehrabian | Interest / Engagement | Leaning forward, open torso, direct orientation. |
| **Low Immediacy** | Mehrabian | Boredom / Defense | Leaning back, folded arms, turning away. |
| **Dominance (Power)** | Cuddy / Kinesics | Status / Confidence | Expanded posture, hands on hips, asymmetrical limbs. |
| **Submissiveness** | Birdwhistell | Vulnerability / Fear | Shoulders hunched, limbs close to body, neck protected. |
| **Congruence** | Mehrabian | Honesty | Alignment of head, torso, and toes toward subject. |
| **Incongruence** | Leakage Theory | Hidden Intent | Head and body orientation conflict (e.g., feet toward exit). |

## 3. Integrated Illustration Specifications
Composite attitudes for complex character states:

1. **The Skeptical Observer**: Contempt (Face) + Crossed arms, weight on back leg (Body).
2. **The Defensive Aggressor**: Anger/Fear blend (Face) + Puffed chest with clenched hands near midline (Body).
3. **The Vulnerable Supplicant**: Sadness/Fear blend (Face) + Hunched shoulders and open visible hands (Body).
